# Law of large numbers (theory)

## What it is

Sample averages converge to the true expected value as n grows. More data gives better estimates of the mean.

## When to use it

To justify long history for stable estimates (mean return, vol, win rate). To understand why backtests and Monte Carlo improve with more data or paths.

## How it works

Concept only; no runner. Use when choosing sample size. Assumes stationarity and finite variance.

## Related tests

Summary stats; Monte Carlo; rolling window backtests.

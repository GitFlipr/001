# Gann Square of Nine

## What it is

Gann tool for price and time; outputs price levels and time pivots.

## When to use it

For Gann-based analysis with Gann angles and Fibonacci.

## How it works

Apply Square of Nine to price and time; output levels and pivots. Custom or Gann libs.

## Inputs

Price and time; config.

## Outputs

Levels, pivots; optionally plots. Location: results/, logs/.

## Related tests

Gann angles; Fibonacci; swing detection; statistical significance.

# Gann angles

## What it is

Lines from price/time pivots at fixed slopes (e.g. 1x1 = 45 degrees). Used in Gann theory for trend and support/resistance.

## When to use it

For Gann-based strategies. To compare with Fibonacci levels. With swing detection for pivot points.

## How it works

From pivot (price, time), draw lines at defined angles/slopes. Output: angle lines or levels. Tools: custom.

## Inputs

Pivot points (e.g. from swing_detection). Config: angles/slopes.

## Outputs

Angle lines or levels; optionally plots. Location: results/, logs/.

## Related tests

Swing detection; Fibonacci; Gann Square of Nine; statistical significance.
